The Four Seasons

Spring
Life starts in the spring as plants grow new leaves. Spring training hints at the start of baseball season.
Summer
Baseball season. The hottest time of year occurs during the backend of this season.
Autumn
Also known as "fall", our planet shows off its variety of colors.
Winter
A season that we know is coming. Snow and ice are a present danger in some areas.
